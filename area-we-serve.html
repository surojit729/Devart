Local Zoning and Land Use Laws (Building Heights, Setbacks, and Land Coverage)

One of the crucial considerations in any Millbrae home remodeling project is understanding and adhering to local zoning and land use laws.

  • Building Heights the maximum building height for residential properties is usually 30 feet. This height limit ensures that buildings harmonize with the neighborhood character and preserve views of surrounding properties.
  • Setbacks are the minimum distance a building or structure must be from the property line. In Millbrae, front yard setbacks are typically 20 feet, side yard setbacks are 5 feet, and rear yard setbacks are 15 feet.
  • Land Coverage includes all areas of a property covered by structures, including the main building, garages, decks, and other accessory structures. The maximum lot coverage for residential properties in Millbrae is usually 40% of the total lot area.

Costs and Fees

The cost of a home remodeling project in Millbrae can vary widely depending on its scope, size, and complexity. Here is a general breakdown of potential costs for typical remodeling projects:

  • Building Permit (Single-family/Duplex):
    • Minor Kitchen/Bathroom Remodel: $300-$600
    • Major Remodels (structural changes, roof framing): $600-$1,800+ (depending on complexity)
    • Additions > 500 sq ft: $1,800+
    • New Construction: $2,500+
  • Planning Permit: $300-$1,200+ (required for significant changes, historic districts, PD zoning)
  • Electrical Permit: $120-$360+ (depending on scope)
  • Plumbing Permit: $120-$240+ (depending on scope)
  • Other Permits: May be required for landscaping, pools, HVAC systems, etc. (costs vary).
