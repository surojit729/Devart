Milpitas Pool and Backyard Remodel

There are many ways to customize your Milpitas pool and backyard remodel project to ensure you have a one-of-a-kind outdoor space for your family.

DevArt 8 does everything outdoors – from landscape design, concrete, and pavers to swimming pools, spas, patios, driveways, outdoor kitchens, walkways, fireplaces, water features, and more!

Whether you seek a modest enhancement for your front or backyard or have a plain landscape that needs a full design, let our experience and creativity work to create your dream outdoor space.

Modern Outdoor Space Features for Your Home

Working with DevArt 8 guarantees several options for creating an at-home oasis, from designing the space itself to complementing it with a garden/landscaping. The most popular services include:

1

Pool Construction

From classic water-line tile and lap pools to modern vanishing edge pools with rock caves, slides, and waterfalls, we can bring your pool and spa construction vision to life.

2

Patio, Decks and Driveways

Transform your outdoor living spaces with our well-designed patios, decks, and a range of durable driveway options, such as pavers and other quality materials. You will get an inviting environment that blends with your property, ensuring longevity and minimal upkeep.

3

Landscaping Styles

From landscape design and xeriscaping to creating stunning gardens, water features, and installing efficient irrigation systems, you can add beauty and sustainability to your outdoor space.

4

Hardscaping Services

Upgrade your outdoor spaces with a range of options, including well-crafted pavers, retaining walls, and inviting walkways. All are meant to ensure you have functional outdoor living spaces that integrate with your landscape, ensuring a harmonious and visually appealing outdoor environment that meets your unique needs.

5

Firepits and Fireplaces

Experience the warmth and ambiance of outdoor fire features, such as fire pits, fire bowls, and fireplaces. The services include installing permanent or semi-permanent structures, connecting gas lines or installing gas tanks, and adhering to local building codes and safety regulations.

6

Outdoor Kitchen

Based on your needs, you get a comprehensive range of services ranging from simple prefabricated grilling islands to fully-equipped culinary spaces. These kitchens include refrigerators, sinks, cabinets, ample storage, versatile grills with side burners and griddles, wood-fired pizza ovens, or even dedicated cocktail stations.

7

Artificial Turfs & Courts/Synthetic Grass

Transform your outdoor spaces with lush, low-maintenance artificial turf and court installations. Enjoy vibrant lawns, versatile sport courts, and safe playground turf, all crafted with high-quality materials to enhance your outdoor experience.

The Pool Construction and Yard Upgrade Process

Whether it is a landscape redesign, new outdoor features, pool construction or building structures, we ensure you are involved throughout the process/p>

Phase Description
Site Visit and Analysis
  • - In-depth site analysis
  • - Project goal discussion
  • - Data collection (measurements, elevations, drainage, etc.)
Design and Obtaining Permits
  • - Design sketches based on analysis
  • - Plant and product recommendations
  • - Preliminary 3D rendering
  • - Applying and getting permits
  • - Inspection coordination with authorities
Construction (Pool and Yard)
  • - Excavation and Earthwork
  • - Pool Base Construction
  • - Steel Cage Reinforcement
  • - Plumbing and Electrical Installations
  • - Shotcreting
  • - Waterproofing
  • - Interior Finish
  • - Construction of Coping
Final Yard Checks
  • - Thorough clean-up
  • - Yard debris removal
  • - Driveway power washing
  • - Leave property in pristine condition
