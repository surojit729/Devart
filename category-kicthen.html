Open-Concept Kitchen Floor Plan Upgrade

Open-concept kitchen remodels involve changing your kitchen layout to create an open, airy space connected to other areas of your home by knocking down walls and finishing smooth floor transitions.

With design and structural considerations, open-concept kitchen remodels are often characterized by clean, simple lines and minimal trimming. We choose and install cabinets and countertops with sleek, straightforward designs and go for handleless cabinets to further streamline the look.

Consider adding windows or skylights to create a bright, open space with natural lighting. For further detailing, we can guide you through natural colors, open shelving, and minimalist hardware choices.

Cost to Remodel Your Kitchen

A kitchen remodel in Milpitas can cost anywhere between $10,000 and $50,000 or even higher, depending on numerous factors. Understanding the factors that influence the cost of a kitchen renovation is crucial for homeowners who want to set a realistic budget.

Let's break down the average kitchen renovation cost in Milpitas based on factors like scope of the project, materials and finishes, size of kitchen, labor cost and more.

  • Cabinetry and Hardware: 29-35% of the budget
  • Countertops: 10-20%
  • Appliances: 10-15%
  • Flooring: 7-10%
  • Plumbing and Fixtures: 8-10%
  • Electrical and Lighting: 5-8%
  • Design and Labor: 15-20%
  • Contingency (unforeseen expenses): 5-10%
